Lasgana Recipe

Easy Homemade Lasagna

Ingredients

  • lasagn noodles
  • shredded mozerella
  • parmesan cheese
  • ricotta cheese
  • eggs
  • salt
  • tomato sauce

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven
  2. mix cheese and eggs
  3. spread 1/3 of tomato sauce and cheese mix
  4. top with 3 lasagna noodles
  5. repeat steps 3 and 4 until
  6. top with last of cheese mix
  7. cover and bake for 45 minutes
  8. uncover and back for another 15 minutes
  9. let rest and enjoy
